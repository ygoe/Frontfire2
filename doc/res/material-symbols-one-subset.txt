Material Symbols font
=====================
This is a subset of the full symbols collection.
Multiple styles and weights are combined in one font file.

HTML usage
----------
<i class="material">NAME</i>

Suffixes in NAME
----------------
(none) = line style, weight 400 (regular)
.7 = line style, weight 700 (bold)
.fill = filled style, weight 400 (regular)
